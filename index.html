Questions I hear often

What does Original Medicare actually cover?

Medicare Parts A and B cover a lot, but not everything. Understanding the gaps is the first step.

Do I need life insurance if I'm retired?

It depends on your situation. Final expenses, remaining debts, and family needs all factor in.

What's the difference between Plan G and Plan N?

Both are popular Medigap plans. The trade-off is between monthly cost and small copayments.

What is hospital indemnity insurance?

It pays you a fixed cash amount when you're hospitalized. You can use it for anything you need.
